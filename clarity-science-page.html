What is Nano-Emulsion Technology?

Traditional THC edibles have a major flaw: they're poorly absorbed by your body. THC is fat-soluble, meaning it doesn't dissolve in water or blood. When you eat a regular edible, your liver has to process it through "first-pass metabolism," which destroys up to 80% of the THC before it ever reaches your bloodstream.

Clarity's nano-emulsion technology changes everything.

We break THC molecules down into nanoparticles—less than 200 nanometers in size—and make them water-soluble. This allows them to bypass slow digestion and absorb directly through your mucous membranes and stomach lining.

  • 85% bioavailability (vs. 4-20% for regular edibles)
  • Effects in 5-15 minutes (vs. 30-120 minutes)
  • Predictable, consistent results every time
