Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I feel the effects?

Unlike traditional edibles that can take 60-120 minutes, our nano-emulsion technology delivers effects in just 5-15 minutes. This allows you to gauge your response in real-time and dose accordingly.

What's the difference between 5mg and 10mg?

5mg is perfect for beginners or those who prefer microdosing for gentle relaxation. 10mg is our standard strength for experienced users seeking stronger effects for pain management, sleep, or deeper relaxation.

Can I take Clarity capsules daily?

Yes! Many of our users incorporate Clarity into their daily wellness routine, similar to taking a vitamin or supplement. Start with one capsule and adjust based on your needs.

How long do the effects last?

Clarity capsules provide 4-8 hours of sustained effects, depending on dosage and individual metabolism. The 5mg Starter typically lasts 4-6 hours, while the 10mg Regular lasts 6-8 hours.

Are Clarity capsules safe?

All Clarity products are manufactured in GMP-certified facilities and undergo rigorous third-party lab testing. Each batch is tested for potency, purity, and contaminants to ensure pharmaceutical-grade quality.
